Fort Cordoba

Population: ~7,500

Fort Cordoba was founded in 1540 as a naval base aimed at clearing the Pearls of Magdalena of their many pirate nests and it lies at the southernmost point of the mainland. The operation against the pirates proved to be a failure over many decades and the Sirkassian navy soon had to turn its attention elsewhere, leaving the old fort to become a sleepy provincial town over the course of the next one-hundred years.
