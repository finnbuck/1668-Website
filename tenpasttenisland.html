Ten-Past-Ten Island

This island lies close to the end of Gray Bay and is a common stop for sailors making the journey over from the old world. It's main settlement is Hopesend and around a year ago the Iron Coral appeared on its western coast. It is said that a much larger portion of the island used to be settled with small family farms and hamlets, however the middle portion of the island is now covered by a forbidding marsh full of high strangeness. Sages theorise that this may be due to an unkown corrupting substance bleeding through from another dimension.
